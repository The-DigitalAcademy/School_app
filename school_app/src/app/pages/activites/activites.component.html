School Activities

At Shaper High, we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom. Our vibrant array of activities and rich school culture are designed to complement your academic journey and foster a well-rounded educational experience. Whether you’re passionate about sports, the arts, community service, or academic challenges, there’s a place for you here. Our diverse programs offer opportunities to explore new interests, develop leadership skills, and build lasting friendships. From the thrill of athletic competitions to the creativity of our performing arts, each activity is a chance to grow, contribute, and connect.

Sports

Teams: Soccer, basketball, swimming, track, and field, among others. Intramural Sports: Less competitive, offering more inclusive participation. Fitness Programs: Yoga, dance classes, and general fitness training.

Music and Arts

We offer curriculum in art and music typically provides an enhanced and focused educational experience, integrating advanced training in visual arts and music performance with core academic subjects, often including additional opportunities such as masterclasses, exhibitions, and performances.

Extra activities

A special offer curriculum for extracurricular activities includes a diverse range of programs such as chess, debate, robotics, drama, and sports, allowing students to explore their interests, develop new skills, and participate in competitions and events beyond the standard academic curriculum.

Events

Our curriculum for extracurricular activities includes events like cultural day and matric dance, providing students with opportunities to celebrate diversity, engage in community-building activities, and experience significant social milestones as part of their high school experience.
